# Tabero - AI Menu Translation App

  🌏 **Breaking down language barriers, one menu at a time**

  ## Overview

  Tabero is an AI-powered menu translation app designed for travelers. Simply take a photo of any menu, and our advanced AI instantly translates dish names, ingredients, and
  prices into your language.

  ## ✨ Key Features

  - **Instant Translation**: Get results in under 3 seconds
  - **26 Language Support**: Major languages across Asia, Europe, and Americas
  - **Smart Categorization**: Automatically organizes items into appetizers, mains, desserts
  - **Dietary Preferences**: Save and translate your allergies and dietary restrictions
  - **Order History**: Keep track of your favorite dishes from around the world
  - **Multi-Photo Support**: Translate multiple menu pages at once

  ## 🚀 How It Works

  1. **Snap** - Take a photo of any menu
  2. **Translate** - AI processes and translates in seconds
  3. **Order** - Show translated requirements to staff

  ## 📄 Privacy Policy

  We take your privacy seriously.

  **Data Collection**
  - We do not store your photos on our servers
  - Menu photos are processed for translation only and immediately discarded
  - Translation history is stored locally on your device
  - Account information (if any) is securely stored

  **Third-Party Services**
  - We use AI services for translation processing
  - Analytics are collected anonymously to improve app performance
  - No personal data is shared with advertisers

  For full privacy policy, please visit our app settings.
